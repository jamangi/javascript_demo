Viewports

The viewport of a browser is the visible area of the web document.


Ayana can calculate where to move to stay in sight because she knows two things:

  1. Where she is, relative to her canvas.
  2. Where her canvas is, relative to your viewport.

Scrolling

Scroll until Ayana stands beyond one of the colored lines to change the website's color scheme. Click if your screen is too large to scroll any further.

Nowadays every device has a uniquely sized viewport, but you can usually access their custom width and height values through Javascript method calls. Next.
